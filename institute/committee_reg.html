Registration Committee

Committee Responsibilities: 1-Monitor the institute's courses and student retention within from them. Have at least one committee member responsible for each course. 2-Set goals regarding the number of new monthly registrations. 3-Help institute students know their status at the institute regarding the credits and courses they must take to be able to to form. 4-Think of ways to make activities generate more enrollments, plan actions to be carried out before, during or after activities. 5-Develop ways to rescue those who previously attended the institute but currently do not attend. 6-Work with members who are active in the church at an age of institute but who do not attend and helps them to enroll and attend a class, whether in person on Saturday or during the week online.
