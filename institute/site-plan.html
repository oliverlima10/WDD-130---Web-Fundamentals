Activities

Activities Purpose The activities organized by the Institute provide the opportunity for fun in a healthy way, thus creating an environment where you can make and strengthen friendships, share experiences, have fun moments, demonstrate and appreciate other talents, serve and be served. As we learn from the scriptures, … Adam fell that men might be; and men are, that they might have joy. (2 Nephi 2:26).
